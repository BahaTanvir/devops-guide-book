# Chapter 15: The Health Check Headache

> *"A pod running doesn't mean it's ready to serve traffic."*

---

## What You'll Learn

Sarah's service is sending traffic to pods that aren't ready, causing intermittent failures. By the end of this chapter, you'll know how to:

- Design effective liveness, readiness, and startup probes
- Choose the right probe type (HTTP, TCP, exec) for your application
- Tune probe timing (initial delay, period, timeout, thresholds)
- Implement graceful shutdown and connection draining
- Understand how probes interact with pod lifecycle and rolling updates
- Avoid common probe pitfalls that cause cascading failures
